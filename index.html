About Us

Hazel IT Solutions Ltd specializes in providing comprehensive logistics and warehouse management solutions. With over a decade of hands-on experience in the supply chain industry, we deliver expert consulting, implementation, and integration services tailored to your business needs.

Our deep expertise in BlueYonder (formerly JDA/RedPrairie) Warehouse Management Systems, combined with extensive knowledge of third-party logistics operations, positions us as your trusted partner for digital transformation in the warehousing and distribution sector.
